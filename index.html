A drinking card game

Screw the Dealer

Guess the card. Miss twice and you drink the difference. Nail it and the dealer pays.

How to play ▾
  1. The dealer draws a card face-down and the guesser calls its value.
  2. First call right: the dealer drinks 5.
  3. First call wrong: the dealer says higher or lower.
  4. Second call right: the dealer drinks 3.
  5. Second call wrong: the guesser drinks the difference between the call and the card.
  6. The card goes face-up onto the board in its value's spot, then the deal passes left.
