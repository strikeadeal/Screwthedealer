A drinking card game

Screw the Dealer

Guess the card. Miss twice and you drink 1. Nail it and the dealer pays.

How to play ▾
  1. The dealer draws a card face-down and the guesser calls its value.
  2. First call right: the dealer drinks 4.
  3. First call wrong: the dealer says higher or lower.
  4. Second call right: the dealer drinks 2.
  5. Second call wrong: the guesser drinks 1.
  6. The card goes face-up onto the board, and the guess passes left to the next player.
  7. Three missed rounds in a row: a new dealer takes over.
